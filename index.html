About Monica Geller

Monica Geller founded "Cafe Jazz" in 2004. She brought her world-famous cooking skill with a backdrop of live Jazz music in the city of London.Geller was mentored by Marco Pierre White at the beginning of her career in 2002.She completed her Bachelor's degree in Food Science from the University of Dhaka.

Our Recipes

Our menu features a wide range of Mexican, Japanese & Bangladeshi food. Our chief chef Ms.Geller set the recepies herself.Our customers get to choose from this exclusive menu which are freshly cooked by trained chefs in our kitchen.
